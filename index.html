About

Who I Am

Hi! I'm Kamlikar Jashwanth, a developer from Hyderabad, India, passionate about building integrations that connect systems seamlessly and reliably.

I started my career in web development, delivering 25+ real-world projects for clients using Odoo, Wix Studio, and WordPress. Working across these platforms gave me a deep understanding of how applications serve users — and why the data flowing between them matters so much.

That curiosity led me to MuleSoft. I now build REST APIs, DataWeave transformations, database-connected flows, and error-handling systems using Anypoint Studio. I'm actively working toward the MuleSoft Certified Developer (MCD) Level 1 certification.
